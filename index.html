F.A.Q

  • What is Ethical Hacking Day?

    Ethical Hacking Day is a three-day cybersecurity forum hosted by the Cybersecurity & Cyber Intelligence Club at ENSA Tangier. It brings together students, experts, and enthusiasts to discuss the latest trends and innovations in cybersecurity through conferences, workshops, and competitions.

  • When and where will the event take place?

    The event will take place at ENSA Tangier over three days, starting on November 29, 2024.

  • Who can participate in the event?

    Engineering students, cybersecurity professionals, and enthusiasts from various institutions and backgrounds are welcome to participate in Ethical Hacking Day.

  • What is the main objective of the event?

    The objective of the event is to provide a platform for knowledge exchange, innovation, and collaboration in the field of cybersecurity. Participants will engage in a Capture the Flag (CTF) competition, workshops, and networking sessions to enhance their practical skills and broaden their professional networks.

  • How can I register for the event?

    Registration for the event will be available online through the event's official website. Detailed instructions on how to register will be provided closer to the event date.

  • Will there be any prizes for the CTF competition?

    Yes, trophies will be awarded to the winners of the CTF competition, and certificates will be distributed to all participants and speakers.
